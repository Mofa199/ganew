About SCORP

The Standing Committee On Rights and Peace (SCORP) is dedicated to promoting human rights, fostering peace, and ensuring that healthcare is accessible to all, regardless of socioeconomic status, ethnicity, religion, or political affiliation. We work at the intersection of healthcare and human rights, addressing systemic inequalities and advocating for social justice.

SCORP recognizes that health is a fundamental human right and that sustainable peace is essential for healthy communities. Our work encompasses refugee health, conflict resolution, social inclusion, and the elimination of discrimination in healthcare settings. We strive to create a world where every individual has equal opportunity to achieve optimal health and well-being.

Our Guiding Principles

Universal Access

Healthcare is a fundamental human right that should be accessible to all, regardless of background or circumstances.

Equity & Non-Discrimination

Ensuring fair treatment and eliminating all forms of discrimination in healthcare delivery and access.

Dignity & Respect

Treating every individual with dignity and respect, honoring their autonomy and cultural values.

Protection of Vulnerable

Special attention and protection for vulnerable populations including refugees, minorities, and marginalized communities.

Accountability & Transparency

Promoting transparency in healthcare governance and holding systems accountable for human rights obligations.

Participation & Empowerment

Ensuring meaningful participation of communities in health decisions that affect their lives.
