About SCOPE

The Standing Committee On Community Health (SCOPE) is dedicated to promoting health awareness, disease prevention, and community empowerment throughout Tanzania. As one of TAMSA's core committees, SCOPE works tirelessly to bridge the gap between medical education and community health needs.

Our mission centers on creating sustainable health programs that address the most pressing health challenges facing our communities. Through innovative outreach programs, health education initiatives, and collaborative partnerships, SCOPE empowers medical students to become agents of positive change in their communities.
