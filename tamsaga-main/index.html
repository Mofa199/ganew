The Tanzania Medical Students Association (TAMSA) General Assembly is an annual meeting organized by the TAMSA, of which are conducted once in a year since 2010. It is the national body that represents all medical students in Tanzania. The General Assembly provides a platform for medical students to discuss relevant health issues, exchange ideas, and propose solutions to improve the health sector in Tanzania.

The Tanzania Medical Students Association (TAMSA) International Scientific Conference is an annual event organized by TAMSA in collaboration with various Partners. It has been conducted once in a year since 2010. The conference brings together medical students, healthcare professionals, Researchers, and experts from around the world to share and discuss trending healthcare Topics, research findings, innovations, and best practices in Healthcare.

Dear TAMSA Family,

On behalf of the Tanzania Medical Students’ Association (TAMSA), it is with immense honor and heartfelt enthusiasm that I welcome you to participate in the 17th TAMSA International Scientific Conference and 15th General Assembly, scheduled to take place from 4th to 9th October in Dar es salaam, Tanzania.

This prestigious annual gathering brings together medical students, young health professionals, researchers, policymakers, and key stakeholders from across Tanzania, Africa, and beyond, to engage in critical discussions, cutting-edge scientific presentations, leadership exchanges, and policy-shaping forums. As TAMSA, we believe that medical students are not just future healthcare professionals but also present-day changemakers.

This conference is a call to amplify our collective voice and explore solutions to some of the continent’s most pressing health challenges.

Your presence will not only enrich the conversations but also solidify our commitment to inclusivity and unity within the association. This is more than a conference, it is a movement, a gathering of visionaries and dedicated health advocates determined to transform systems, communities, and futures

We look forward to welcoming you to this historic event and to the beautiful city of Dar es salaam.

TAMSA, COMMITTED TO SERVE,

Murtaza

Guest of Honour

We are honored to welcome

Dr. Florence Temu

Country Director, Amref Health Africa Tanzania

We are honored to welcome Dr. Florence Temu, the esteemed Country Director of Amref Health Africa Tanzania, as our Guest of Honour for the 15th General Assembly and 17th International Scientific Conference.

Dr. Temu brings over 20 years of experience in healthcare leadership, program management, and organizational development across Africa. Her progressive career journey from Project Manager to Country Director exemplifies visionary leadership in health systems strengthening.

As a strong advocate for medical education and capacity building, Dr. Temu has consistently supported initiatives that enhance healthcare delivery and professional development across the continent.
