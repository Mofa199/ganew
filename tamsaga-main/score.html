About SCORE

The Standing Committee On Research Exchange (SCORE) is TAMSA's gateway to international collaboration and research excellence. We facilitate meaningful research exchanges, promote collaborative research projects, and provide medical students with opportunities to engage in cutting-edge research both locally and internationally.

Through partnerships with medical institutions across Africa and beyond, SCORE creates pathways for knowledge exchange, capacity building, and research innovation. Our programs are designed to enhance the research skills of medical students while contributing to the global body of medical knowledge.

Exchange Programs & Opportunities

African Research Exchange

Month-long research exchanges with partner institutions across Africa, focusing on continent-specific health challenges and solutions.

Duration: 4-8 weeks Partners: 15+ countries

Laboratory Exchange Program

Hands-on research experience in state-of-the-art laboratories, learning advanced research techniques and methodologies.

Duration: 2-6 weeks Labs: 25+ facilities

Digital Health Research

Virtual research collaborations focusing on digital health innovations, telemedicine, and health informatics.

Duration: Flexible Format: Virtual

Collaborative Research Projects

Multi-institutional research projects addressing major health challenges through collaborative efforts and shared resources.

Duration: 6-12 months Teams: 3-8 institutions

Graduate Research Support

Support for undergraduate research projects, thesis work, and preparation for graduate studies in medical research.

Duration: Variable Support: Full guidance

Research Fellowship Program

Competitive fellowships for outstanding student researchers, providing funding and mentorship for independent research projects.

Duration: 3-6 months Awards: 10+ annually
