Biography

Dr. Florence Temu is the Country Director for Amref Health Africa- Tanzania, a role that she assumed since May 2016. Before that Florence was a Country Director for Amref Health Africa â€“ Ethiopia. While at Amref, Florence rose from being a Project Manager, Head of Programmes, and then Deputy Country Director before becoming a Country Director.

Before joining Amref Health Africa, Florence was the Head of the Cancer Prevention Services Division at Ocean Road Cancer Institute in Tanzania; prior to that, she was a general practitioner and researcher under Maternal and HIV research (PMTCT clinical trials) at Muhimbili National Hospital.
