Session Overview

The Presidents Session is an exclusive leadership forum designed for current and former TAMSA presidents, chapter leaders, and executive committee members. This high-level strategic session focuses on leadership development, organizational governance, and the future vision of medical student representation in Tanzania.

This session provides a platform for experienced leaders to share insights, discuss challenges facing medical student organizations, and develop collaborative strategies for advancing the medical education landscape in Tanzania. It serves as both a learning opportunity and a strategic planning workshop for the next generation of medical leaders.
