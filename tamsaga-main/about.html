Meet the Committee Chairperson's Message

Message from the Chairperson

"It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to our annual General Assembly and International Scientific conference."

Dear Delegates, Esteemed Stakeholders, Partners, and Friends,

With immense joy and overwhelming anticipation, we, the Tanzania Medical Students’ Association (TAMSA), extend our warmest invitation to each of you to join us in the vibrant city of Dar es Salaam, the heart of the Swahili coast, for the 17th International Scientific Conference & 15th General Assembly—a celebration of innovation, leadership, and transformative action in health.

From October 4th to 9th, 2025, Tanzania will become a hub of energy and ideas, uniting hundreds of medical students, healthcare experts, institutions, and stakeholders.

We are standing at the brink of a new era in healthcare—where innovation meets compassion, and artificial intelligence walks hand in hand with human touch. This year’s conference is more than an event—it’s a movement. A convergence of minds from across Tanzania, Africa, and beyond. A fertile ground for knowledge exchange, policy dialogue, research presentations, and collaborative action that will ripple across communities and institutions alike.

We can’t wait to welcome you to Dar es Salaam—where ideas will soar, collaborations will blossom, and lifelong memories will be made.

Suleiman Ngede
Chairperson, GA Organizing Committee
