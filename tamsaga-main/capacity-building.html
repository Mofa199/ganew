About Capacity Building Session

The Capacity Building Session is a cornerstone of the TAMSA General Assembly, designed to strengthen the skills, knowledge, and capabilities of medical students and association members. Led by experienced President Standing Delegates (PSD) and Training Standing Delegates (TSD), this comprehensive program addresses both individual competency development and organizational effectiveness.

This session bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, providing participants with tools and strategies they can immediately implement in their local chapters, academic pursuits, and future medical careers. The training modules are carefully designed to address current challenges while preparing participants for emerging opportunities in healthcare and medical education.

Certification & Recognition

Certification Requirements

  • Attend minimum 80% of scheduled sessions
  • Complete all practical exercises and assignments
  • Pass final assessment with minimum 70% score
  • Submit personal development action plan
  • Participate in peer evaluation and feedback

Recognition Levels
