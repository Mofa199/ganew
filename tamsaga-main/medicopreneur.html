Program Overview

The Medicopreneur Program at TAMSA GA 2025 represents a groundbreaking initiative designed to foster entrepreneurial thinking among medical students and young healthcare professionals. This comprehensive program combines medical expertise with business acumen to create innovative solutions for Africa's healthcare challenges.

Participants will engage in hands-on workshops, mentorship sessions, and pitch competitions that emphasize the practical application of entrepreneurial principles in healthcare settings. The program aims to cultivate a new generation of healthcare leaders who can bridge the gap between medical practice and innovative business solutions.
