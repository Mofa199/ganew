Tournament Overview

The Medical Ethical Moot Court is a prestigious competition that brings together medical students from across Tanzania to engage in legal reasoning, ethical analysis, and advocacy skills through simulated court proceedings focused on complex medical ethics cases.

Competition Format

  • Teams of 3-4 medical students per institution
  • Written submissions due 2 weeks before competition
  • 30-minute oral arguments per team
  • 15-minute Q&A session with judges
  • 5-minute rebuttal period
  • Expert panel deliberation and judgment

Learning Objectives

  • Develop critical thinking in medical ethics
  • Enhance legal reasoning and argumentation skills
  • Practice public speaking and advocacy
  • Understand healthcare law and policy
  • Foster interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Build confidence in professional debates
