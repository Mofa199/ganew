Conference Overview

The 17th International Scientific Conference is the cornerstone event of the TAMSA General Assembly, bringing together the brightest minds in medical research and practice. This two-day scientific symposium features cutting-edge research presentations, innovative case studies, and collaborative discussions on the most pressing health challenges facing Africa and the global community.

Our conference provides a platform for medical students, researchers, healthcare professionals, and policy makers to share knowledge, forge partnerships, and drive innovation in medical science. With over 100 scientific presentations across multiple specialty areas, this is the premier medical research event in East Africa.

Infectious Diseases & Global Health

  • Emerging infectious diseases in Africa
  • Antimicrobial resistance strategies
  • Vaccine development and distribution
  • Global health security and preparedness

Non-Communicable Diseases

  • Cardiovascular disease prevention
  • Diabetes management in resource-limited settings
  • Cancer research and treatment advances
  • Mental health and neurological disorders

Digital Health & Innovation

  • Artificial intelligence in healthcare
  • Telemedicine and remote monitoring
  • Health information systems
  • Mobile health (mHealth) applications

Medical Education & Research

  • Innovative teaching methodologies
  • Research capacity building
  • Student research programs
  • Continuing medical education

  • Poster presentations with guided tours
  • Case study presentations (15 minutes)
  • Research methodology workshops
  • Interactive panel discussions
