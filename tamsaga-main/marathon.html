Event Overview

The TAMSA Medical Marathon is more than just a run - it's a celebration of health, wellness, and community spirit. This charity event brings together medical students, healthcare professionals, and health enthusiasts to raise awareness about non-communicable diseases (NCDs) while supporting local healthcare initiatives.

Participants can choose from multiple distance categories, making the event accessible to runners of all fitness levels. All proceeds from the marathon will go towards supporting community health programs and medical education initiatives in Tanzania.

Health & Wellness Fair

Alongside the marathon, we're hosting a comprehensive Health & Wellness Fair featuring:

  • Free health screenings (blood pressure, BMI, blood glucose)
  • NCD awareness and prevention workshops
  • Nutrition counseling and healthy cooking demonstrations
  • Fitness and exercise guidance sessions
  • Mental health and wellness resources
  • First aid training demonstrations
