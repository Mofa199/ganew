Program Overview

The TAMSA Medical Camp is a flagship community outreach program that brings essential healthcare services directly to underserved communities across Tanzania. This initiative combines medical service delivery with valuable learning experiences for medical students and healthcare professionals.

Our medical camp serves as a bridge between the medical community and rural populations, providing free healthcare services while offering students practical experience in community medicine, patient interaction, and healthcare delivery in resource-limited settings.

Services Offered

General Medical Consultation

  • Primary healthcare assessment
  • Common illness diagnosis and treatment
  • Chronic disease management
  • Prescription and medication dispensing

Preventive Care Services

  • Health screenings and vital signs check
  • Blood pressure and diabetes screening
  • Immunization and vaccination services
  • Health education and awareness sessions

Maternal & Child Health

  • Prenatal and postnatal care
  • Child health assessment and growth monitoring
  • Family planning counseling
  • Nutritional guidance for mothers and children

Specialized Services

  • Basic dental care and oral health
  • Eye examination and vision testing
  • Minor surgical procedures
  • Mental health counseling and support

Learning Objectives for Participants

  • Gain practical experience in community healthcare delivery
  • Develop cultural competency and communication skills with diverse populations
  • Understand healthcare challenges in resource-limited settings
  • Practice clinical skills under supervision of experienced physicians
  • Learn about preventive medicine and public health interventions
  • Experience interdisciplinary teamwork in healthcare settings
