Post GA Overview

The Post General Assembly day serves as the perfect conclusion to our intensive week of scientific discourse, governance, and professional development. This celebratory day brings together all participants in a relaxed atmosphere to reflect on achievements, forge lasting friendships, and experience the rich cultural heritage of Tanzania.

More than just a social gathering, Post GA provides opportunities for informal networking, knowledge sharing, and the building of collaborative relationships that will extend far beyond the conference. It's where lifelong professional partnerships are formed and where the true spirit of TAMSA community shines brightest.
