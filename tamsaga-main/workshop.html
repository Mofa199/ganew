Program Overview

The Training with AENON program is a specialized emergency medicine training initiative designed for medical students and young healthcare professionals. This intensive program provides hands-on experience in emergency care procedures, critical decision-making, and life-saving interventions.

Participants will receive internationally recognized Continuing Professional Development (CPD) credits upon successful completion of the program. The training is conducted by experienced emergency medicine specialists from AENON Healthcare, ensuring high-quality education and practical skills development.

Training Modules

Basic Life Support (BLS)

  • CPR techniques and protocols
  • Airway management
  • Use of AED devices
  • Team-based resuscitation

Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS)

  • Advanced airway management
  • Cardiac rhythm interpretation
  • Pharmacology in emergency care
  • Post-cardiac arrest care

Trauma Management

  • Primary and secondary assessment
  • Hemorrhage control techniques
  • Fracture stabilization
  • Shock management protocols

Pediatric Emergency Care

  • Pediatric assessment techniques
  • Age-specific emergency protocols
  • Family-centered care approach
  • Child abuse recognition

Learning Outcomes

  • Demonstrate proficiency in basic and advanced life support techniques
  • Perform systematic patient assessment in emergency situations
  • Apply evidence-based emergency medicine protocols
  • Work effectively as part of an emergency response team
  • Communicate effectively with patients, families, and healthcare teams
  • Recognize and manage common emergency conditions
