About SCORA

The Standing Committee On Reproductive Health (SCORA) is dedicated to promoting comprehensive reproductive health education, advocating for reproductive rights, and ensuring access to quality reproductive healthcare services for all individuals in Tanzania and beyond.

SCORA works tirelessly to address reproductive health challenges through evidence-based interventions, peer education programs, and advocacy initiatives. Our mission centers on reducing maternal and infant mortality, preventing sexually transmitted infections, and empowering individuals to make informed decisions about their reproductive health.

Our Focus Areas

Sexual & Reproductive Education

  • Comprehensive sexuality education programs
  • Peer-to-peer education initiatives
  • Family planning counseling and guidance
  • Adolescent reproductive health awareness

Maternal & Child Health

  • Antenatal and postnatal care promotion
  • Safe delivery and birth spacing advocacy
  • Breastfeeding support and education
  • Prevention of mother-to-child transmission

STI/HIV Prevention

  • HIV/AIDS awareness and prevention campaigns
  • STI screening and treatment promotion
  • Voluntary testing and counseling services
  • Behavioral change communication strategies

Reproductive Rights Advocacy

  • Gender equality and women's rights promotion
  • Violence against women prevention programs
  • Access to contraceptives and family planning
  • Policy advocacy for reproductive health
